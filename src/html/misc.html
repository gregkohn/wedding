activities

  • Our favorite thing to do: soak up the sun all day on the beach (Rehoboth, Bethany, Lewes, Dewey, DE state park)
  • Morning yoga & pilates classes on the beach. Schedule. (Bethany)
  • Kayak, SUP, fish, swim, hike, bike at Delaware State Park about a 30 min drive from the beach. $10 fee per car.
  • Shell We Golf? Mini golf, ice cream, gift store and free samples of fudge. (Rehoboth)
  • The sea dragon ride at Funland, the best $2 you’ll ever spend (Rehoboth)

dining

  • La Fable is the restaurant we dined at after Greg proposed. It’s walkable from the Rehoboth boardwalk. Make a reservation, as the space is small and they get really busy during beach season. (Rehoboth)
  • BlueCoast has great seafood and drinks. They are part of a larger restaurant group that has locations throughout the area’s beaches.
  • This is one of the spots we checked out for our wedding! Big Chill Beach Club is a scenic, cool place to eat, drink and hang out. (Bethany)
  • Hit up the bars and restaurants in Dewey

shopping

  • Shop tax-free at Tanger outlets. There are three locations on either side of Highway 1: Seaside, Bayside and Surfside.(Rehoboth)

other

  • If you have some time, take a free tour of the Dogfish Head brewery, a 40-minute drive away in Milton. If not, they have a couple restaurants at Rehoboth. (Milton, Rehoboth)
  • Check out the Visit Delaware site for various recommendations at all the nearby beaches.
